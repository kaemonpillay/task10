About Us

Who Are We?

We are a new, up and coming polictial party operating acorss South Africa. Established in 2023, our founders are majority youth-aged with a range between 25-30 years old. We have decided to create this party and compete in elections because we are tired of being passer-bys when there are serious issues affecting our streets daily. We are a dedicated, and focused group of individuals workings together with our community to raise awareness of the issues we face every day and creating solutions together. Our party is based on togetherness and with the belief that one person cannot change our country, but rather the efforts of the collective citizens.

Our Objectives

  1. Reduce the crime rate in the areas we operate in then move outwards to the next districts
  2. Create communities that are helpful and support one another
  3. Create more public facilities including parks and outside gyms
  4. Build more public schools and learning facicilities for children
  5. Erect a new library
  6. Ensure cleaner streets and roads that are pothole free
  7. Ensure that streetlamps are never non-functional
